November Ministry Update Blake C Pfaff

Laying the Groundwork & Texas Time

This month I went to Fort Collins, CO for New Staff Training. Usually, there is about a 2:1 ratio of girls to guys, but this year we were blessed with having an all guys NST. What I love about StuMo is how evangelism is always the focus in everything we do, even when discipling students. The only way I can spend so much time on campus meeting new guys and sharing the gospel with them is through my support team. Thank y'all!

I want to be faithful in every city I'm at, whether Tucson or back in Collin County. I've been able to speak to Plano Senior High School before their games as a chaplain multiple times this fall, which has been so fun. I've talked about the gospel, having a heart for God, and the combination of suffering and joy that comes from living for Jesus.

What's Going on at Arizona?

Would you believe me if I told you the isolation and disruption COVID has brought actually grew a curiosity and hunger for faith in the students at Arizona more than ever before? The time to share and walk with students is NOW! There has been 4 freshman who have given their lives to Christ just in the Sig Ep fraternity.
41 students who have signed up to go SMC, our winter conference, in Frisco this year! Many of the students who accepted Christ at this conference in previous years are now leading others to Christ.
BOOK OF THE MONTH
When I came to Christ I desperately wanted to know the absolute truth about God. How do we know the Bible is true Word of God? Why are the Gospels the most reliable source of Jesus's life? Is there evidence to back up the Christian faith?

I should have read this book sooner in my walk with Christ. It has such great information to those who want to talk with friends who have an intellectual barrier to Christ.

"The Case for Christ" by Lee Strobel is a must read for people wanting answers to tough questions, both believers and nonbelievers.

Thank you donors and prayer team!

Here are some prayer points you can join me in praying for!

  • My former football teammates to start having a desire to learn about God, so when I come in January it is the perfect time
  • I am 64% funded, praise God! Be praying for generous hearts and for donors to come on to my financial team
  • The Arizona students going to SMC (our winter conference) to be healthy and for the things they learn about Christ to take root in their hearts.

Thank you for being a part of my ministry!
